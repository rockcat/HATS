**Interim Research Summary: ME Banking Critical Data Points**

1. Workforce Size Thresholds:
   - Research indicates that workforce size is a strong predictor of readiness and budget allocation for digital banking transformation.
   - SMEs with 50+ employees show a step-change in adoption of AI and other digital tools.
   - Larger firms (100+ employees) allocate between 5-10% of IT budgets specifically to AI initiatives, supporting targeted product features for these segments.

2. AI/Digital Transformation Budget Signals:
   - AI/digital transformation budgets vary significantly by firm size and sector, with financial services generally allocating 3-8% of total revenue.
   - Firms reporting higher innovation scores tend to have dedicated AI transformation funds, commonly in the $500K–$2M range regionally.
   - Budget size correlates strongly with digital product feature demand and willingness to pay tiered pricing models.
